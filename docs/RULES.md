# House Rules & Content Notes

This app is a **companion tool**, not a rules reference. It deliberately contains
**no official rulebook text, card text, artwork, or proprietary assets** from
*Deep Rock Galactic: The Board Game* or its expansions.

## How content works here

- All mission types, warnings, anomalies, biomes, and shop effects use
  **generic placeholder names** that you can rename.
- Shop items are **fully editable** (label, currency, cost, enabled, effect
  note) per campaign.
- Rules reminders are **user-authored notes**, not copied card text.
- Bring your own physical game for the authoritative rules and components.

## Economy layer (optional house rule)

The Economy and Deeper Dive modes are fan-style house rules:

- Dwarves carry HP/ammo/gear between missions.
- Gold/Nitra collected after a mission feed a team pool (with an even Gold
  split to escaped dwarves; remainder to the pool).
- Between missions, spend at the rig shop.
- Unspent Gold/Nitra are confiscated at launch (configurable).
- Left-behind dwarves take a configurable penalty (soft / harsh / custom).

Edit any of this under House Rules — nothing here is mandatory.
